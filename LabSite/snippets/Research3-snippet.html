Positive Outlook on Women Entering Reproductive treatments (POWER)

This longitudinal study seeks to identify the multiple trajectories of mental health (well-being and distress) and personal growth from the start of fertility treatment through pregnancy and motherhood, examining the unique and combined contribution of background, medical, psychological, and environmental variables to explaining the heterogeneity in mental health and growth.
