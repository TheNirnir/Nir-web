Oneg Kabizon

Oneg Kabizon

is an MA student in the Clinical Social Work program at the Louis and Gabi Weisfeld School of Social Work at the Bar-Ilan University. She is a Bachelor of Social Work from the Sapir Academic College. She is currently working at Reuth medical center with young men and women with physical disabilities, who deal with sexual and romantic challenges. In addition, she works with young men and women (18-25) at Tel Aviv's Municipal Social Services. Her MA thesis, under the supervision of Dr. Shirley Ben Shlomo, deals with the formation of personal identity, and with the quality of life satisfaction among young national religious Israeli women.
