Esther Joffe

Esther Joffe

is a social worker. She has a BA in Social Work from the Louis and Gabi Weisfeld School of Social Work at Bar-Ilan University. She is currently working as a social worker for teenage girls in the Ethiopian community in Bet Shemesh. She is the research manager for the longitudinal mothers' of twins and single babies study (MOST) being conducted by the lab.
