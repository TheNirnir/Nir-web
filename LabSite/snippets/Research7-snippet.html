Terror Management Theory (TMT) in Everyday Life

This series of studies draws on TMT to examine a wide range of social and personal responses following exposure to thoughts about death, including: risk taking, perception of meaning in life, maternal separation anxiety, attitudes towards people with disabilities, and interpersonal relationships.

Example articles:
