Meital Navon

Meital Navon

is a PhD candidate at the Louis and Gabi Weisfeld School of Social Work at Bar-Ilan University, under the supervision of Prof. Orit Taubman – Ben-Ari. Meital is a clinical social worker and graduated her MA at the Louis and Gabi Weisfeld School of Social Work at Bar-Ilan University. She has a BA in Social Work from the Hebrew University in Jerusalem, and has studied animal-assisted therapy. She currently works with adolescents and youth at risk in Jerusalem. Her MA thesis, under the supervision of Prof. Orit Taubman – Ben-Ari, focused on affect regulation processes and their association with risky driving.
