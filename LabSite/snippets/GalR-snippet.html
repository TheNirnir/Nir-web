Gal Rozen

Gal Rozen

is a clinical social worker and a PhD candidate under the supervision of Prof. Orit Taubman – Ben-Ari. He has a BA in Philosophy and a BA and Master’s degree in Social Work from Bar-Ilan university, and he specializes in working with children and their parents and team training. He currently serves as the coordinator of clinical work at the Neve Hanna Children’s Home for youngsters from troubled families in Kiryat Gat. His Master’s thesis, under the supervision of Prof. Orit Taubman – Ben-Ari, dealt with personal growth among mothers of pre-term babies, examining the contribution of the health risk to the infants, the perceived stress of the mothers, and the emotional support of the grandmother.
