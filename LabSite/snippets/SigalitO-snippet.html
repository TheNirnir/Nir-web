Sigalit Offer

Sigalit Offer

is a clinical social worker and a PhD candidate. She is a lecturer at the Louis and Gabi Weisfeld School of Social Work of Bar-Ilan University. She is also a certified couple and family therapist. For more than 23 years, she worked at Schneider Children’s Medical Center of Israel in the Department of Premature and Newborns and at the Psychiatric Department of Eating Disorders. Today she works at the unit for Child Development. Her research, under the supervision of Prof. Orit Taubman – Ben-Ari, focuses on the meaning of giving birth to a premature baby on the motherhood experience in retrospect.
