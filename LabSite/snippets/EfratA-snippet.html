Efrat Amitay

Efrat Amitay

is an MA student in the Clinical Social Work program at the Louis and Gabi Weisfeld School of Social Work at Bar-Ilan University. She has a BA in Social Work from the Academic College of Judea and Samaria in Ariel, and is currently working as a juvenile probation officer in Jerusalem, working with youngsters who have committed criminal offenses. Her MA thesis, under the supervision of Prof. Orit Taubman – Ben-Ari, deals with the contribution of personal, social, and family characteristics on youngsters’ driving before they actually receive their license.
